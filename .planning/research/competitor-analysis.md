## Market Insights

### Common Patterns (Industry Standards)
- Most tutoring sites feature prominent "Book Now" CTAs
- Testimonials are ubiquitous (social proof is critical)
- Many use Calendly or similar for booking
- Pricing transparency varies widely

### Gaps in the Market (Opportunities)
- Few competitors have student portals
- Limited use of video on websites (explainer videos, tutor intros)
- Most lack comprehensive FAQ sections
- Many have outdated designs (opportunity to stand out)

### Emerging Trends
- AI-powered practice tools (e.g., personalized problem generators)
- Hybrid models (in-person + online options)
- Subscription-based pricing (vs. pay-per-session)
- Parent communication apps (session notes, progress tracking)

## Templates for Competitor Research

### Mystery Shopping Script
> "Hi, I'm looking for tutoring help for my [grade]-grade [son/daughter] in [subject]. Can you tell me about your availability and pricing?"

**What to observe**:
- Response time
- Professionalism
- Pricing clarity
- Next steps (do they push for a call? Email? Booking link?)
