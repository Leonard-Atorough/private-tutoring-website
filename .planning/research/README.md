# Research

This folder contains user research, competitive analysis, and market insights that inform product decisions.

## Folder Contents

### Competitive Analysis
- **`competitor-analysis.md`** - Review of similar tutoring websites
- **`market-trends.md`** - Educational technology trends and insights
- **`pricing-research.md`** - Market rates and pricing models

### User Research
- **`user-interviews/`** - Notes and findings from parent/student interviews
- **`usability-tests/`** - Test results and observations
- **`surveys/`** - Survey responses and analysis
- **`personas.md`** - User personas based on research

### Analytics
- **`analytics-reports/`** - Monthly analytics summaries
- **`heatmaps/`** - Hotjar/Clarity session recordings and heatmap insights
- **`conversion-funnel.md`** - Analysis of user journey and drop-off points

## Research Guidelines

### When to Add Research
- Before making major UX decisions
- When considering new features
- Quarterly reviews of user behavior
- After receiving significant user feedback

### Research Document Template

```markdown
# [Research Title]

**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD  
**Researcher**: Name  
**Method**: Interview / Survey / Usability Test / Analytics  

## Research Question
What are we trying to learn?

## Methodology
How did we conduct this research?

## Participants
Who did we research? (Demographics, sample size)

## Key Findings
1. Finding one
2. Finding two
3. Finding three

## Insights & Recommendations
What actions should we take based on this research?

## Raw Data
Link to detailed notes, recordings, or data files.
```
