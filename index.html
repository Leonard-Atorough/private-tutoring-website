ABOUT ME

Dedicated to helping students succeed in Maths and Science

I'm Kaili, a PHD Holder in Nanochemistry and your next Maths and Science tutor.

I specialise in teaching Primary and Scondary level Maths, Chemistry and Physics and University level Maths and Chemistry.

With over 8 years of teaching experience and a passion for education, I help students unlock their full potential in mathematics and sciences. My approach combines rigorous academic understanding with practical problem-solving techniques, ensuring that complex concepts become accessible and engaging. Whether you're aiming to improve your grades, prepare for exams, or deepen your understanding of these subjects, I provide personalized support tailored to your learning style and goals. My students consistently achieve improved grades and, more importantly, develop confidence in their abilities that extends beyond the classroom.
