Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Kaili Stacey Tutoring ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (https://kailistutoring.netlify.app).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Form Data: Name, email address, subject, and message when you use our contact form
  • Booking Information: Google account information when booking sessions through Google Calendar

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: Page views, referral source, device type, browser, and general location (anonymized) via Plausible Analytics
  • IP Address: Your IP address when accessing Google Fonts

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To process and confirm session bookings
  • To understand how visitors use our website and improve user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

We share personal data with the following third-party service providers. We do not sell or intentionally share your data for marketing purposes:

5. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data on our servers. Here's how your data is handled:

  • Contact Forms: Immediately forwarded to FormSubmit.co; we do not store copies
  • Booking Data: Stored in your Google Calendar account per Google's retention policy
  • Analytics: Retained by Plausible Analytics per their data retention policy (typically 30 days for raw data)
  • Error & Performance Data: Error reports, performance traces, and session replay data sent to Sentry are retained according to our Sentry project settings and Sentry's retention policy. We sample transactions at ~20% for performance monitoring, record ~10% of normal sessions for session replay and 100% of sessions that generate errors for replay. See Sentry's privacy policy for details.

To request deletion of your personal data, please contact us at kailistacey@gmail.com.

Sentry & Sensitive Data

We use Sentry for error tracking and debugging. Sentry may collect technical information (errors, stack traces, breadcrumbs, performance metrics, and (where enabled) session replay). We do not intentionally log sensitive information (passwords, payment details, or full personal identifiers). If you believe sensitive data was included in an error report, please contact us at kailistacey@gmail.com and we will investigate and take appropriate steps to remove it.

Opt-out / Contact

If you would like to opt out of error tracking or session replay for your sessions, contact us at kailistacey@gmail.com and we will provide available options.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correct: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of analytics tracking or contact communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at kailistacey@gmail.com.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no transmission of data over the internet or storage method is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and secure connections.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Plausible Analytics: We use Plausible Analytics for website analytics. Plausible does not use cookies and is fully GDPR compliant. It collects only essential, anonymized data about your visit.

Google Services: Google Fonts and Google Calendar may use cookies in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your home country. By using our website, you consent to such transfers.

11. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing personal information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of our website constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
